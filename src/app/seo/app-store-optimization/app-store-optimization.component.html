What is App Store Optimization?
App Store Optimization (ASO) is the process of improving an app's visibility within the app stores (such as the App Store for iOS and Google Play for Android) and increasing app conversion rates. The primary goals of ASO are to boost app rankings in search results, enhance visibility across top categories, and improve click-through rates for downloads.
