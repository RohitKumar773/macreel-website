What is Technical SEO?
Technical SEO involves optimizing the infrastructure of your website to ensure that search engines can easily crawl, index, and rank your pages. It focuses on the technical aspects of your site, such as site speed, mobile-friendliness, security, and architecture, to ensure that your website meets the latest search engine requirements and provides a seamless user experience.
