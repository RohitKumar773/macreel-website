What is Hybrid App Development?
Hybrid App development combines the best of both worlds—native and web applications. By leveraging a single codebase, hybrid apps are designed to run on various platforms, such as iOS, Android, and Windows, while maintaining a native-like experience. This approach not only accelerates the development process but also reduces costs and ensures a consistent user experience across different devices.
